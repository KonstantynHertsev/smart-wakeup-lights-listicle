Questions worth asking before you buy

Does a sunrise alarm actually work, or is it a gimmick? +

For most people, yes — gradually rising light suppresses melatonin before the alarm sound hits, so you wake up less groggy. The effect is strongest with lights that ramp for 20–30 minutes rather than 5.

Do I still need a sound alarm as backup? +

If you're a deep sleeper, keep a sound layer on for the first few weeks. Every light on this list pairs light with an audio chime once the ramp finishes.

What if the power goes out overnight? +

Only three of the five hold a charge through an outage. We flagged battery backup in the table above — check it if your power is unreliable.
