How It Actually Works

Microcurrent devices send a very low-level electrical current through the skin, at a strength well below what you'd feel as a shock — most people describe it as a faint tingle, if anything. The working theory is that it prompts a small ATP response in facial muscle tissue, which is the same mechanism behind most clinical "toning" facials. It's not a replacement for sunscreen or sleep. It's a five-minute habit that compounds.

I still have tired mornings. I just don't need three products to hide them anymore.
